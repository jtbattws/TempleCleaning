Attire & Preparation Guidelines

Please review these details carefully before your scheduled shift to ensure a smooth check-in process at the temple.

  • Clothing: Wear clean, comfortable white or light-colored clothing suitable for physical work. Modest Sunday attire is also completely acceptable if changing rooms are utilized.
  • Footwear: Please bring clean, soft-soled shoes or white slippers to change into inside the temple. Heavy or outside shoes are restricted in carpeted areas.
  • Arrival Time: Arrive exactly 10–15 minutes early at the designated volunteer entrance to receive assignments and pass security check-in.
